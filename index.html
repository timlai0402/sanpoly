About Sanpoly

Delivering reliable woven packaging solutions with strong manufacturing capabilities.

Overview

Sanpoly Sdn Bhd is a premier woven packaging manufacturer in Malaysia, serving industries such as fertilizers, feedmills, palm oleochemicals, and construction sectors.

Company Philosophy

Our core philosophy focuses on delivering complete customer packaging solutions based on our 3P principle:

✓

Protect – Ensuring product safety

✓

Position – Strengthening brand presence

✓

Performs – Delivering efficiency and durability

Our team works together to bring this vision into reality, supporting a diverse and growing global customer base.
