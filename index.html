About Us

About Sanpoly

Overview

Sanpoly Industry Sdn. Bhd. (Sanpoly) has over 20 years of experience in manufacturing, supplying, importing and exporting packaging products as a leading firm in Malaysia. Over the years, we have consistently provided high-quality packaging solutions to the agricultural, human food, and animal feed industries, building strong trust and confidence among our customers.

Company Description

Sanpoly is a large-scale enterprise with a 3.5-acre manufacturing plant located in Melaka, strategically positioned between Selangor and Johor. We specialise in the production of PP woven bags, BOPP laminated bags, paper/PE woven laminated bags, bulk bags, sling bags, and PP woven fabrics.

Our products serve a wide range of industries including fertilizer, rice, wheat flour, sugar, animal feed, pet food, and construction materials. We are committed to delivering reliable packaging solutions that meet industrial standards and customer expectations.
